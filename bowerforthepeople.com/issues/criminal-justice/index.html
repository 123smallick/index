Transforming our criminal (in)justice system

It is clear that our country's immigration system has been built to favor the wealthy and the powerful. It is no secret. For years, politicians, citizens, and activists have known that our systems for criminal justice disproportionately punishes the poorest and most powerless in our country, yet the current adminstration fails to take any action to ameliorate this injustice.

The conservative-led War on Drugs has done nothing but create disastrous results and destroy commmunities. Incarceration results directly from the corrupt cash bail system and legal fees. The for-profit prison industry continues to destroy the pillars of justice that our nation used to stand beside. –

Bower will meaningully address these backwards policies by dismantling and completely reimagining the structures of our criminal justice system. To rebuild this fundamentally broken system, the American people must undertake a massive decriminalization and decarceration effort to construct a system that values equality, liberty, safety, and dignity.
