Confronting the climate crisis

Climate change is real. The people must have the power to stop greedy, corrupt corporations from consuming the beauty of our planet. Climate change's destructive effects are already our reality and, disproportionately, minority communities have been at the most risk. Hurricanes, unprecedented natural disasters, extreme temperatures, and deteriorating Water and air, continue to threaten our safety, create billions in economic burdens, endanger national health, and exacerbate economic disparities.

The current administration vehemently denies the impacts of climate change, while simultaneously eliminating environmental protections, inviting companies to gut protected lands for private exploitation, and pushing profits above the safety of the planet and our people. Small restrictions and policies won't fix anything. It's time for new, radical policy to protect our communities.

Bower's adminstration will push an augmented version of the Green New Deal, which will fundamentally advance our political and socioeconomic systems to create a safer, greener environment across America.

We can no longer pretend that climate change's effects are simply isolated incidents –. Our response to the climate crisis must be centered around providing for our children and protecting their future. We comprehensively address the challenge of our century, preparing for the natural disaster, and increasing access to clean air, clean water, and clean environments for all of our communities.
