An economy that works for everyone

Today, more than ever, America is being torn apart from systemic, deeply rooted econmic inequalities. Our country was defined by liberty and freedom. Now, we are seen as a place of endless class war: poor vs rich. Stagnant wages with rising costs, predatory banking practices, higher tuition, expensive child care, unaffordable housing, and the lack of meaninful social safety nets prevents economic mobility and perpetuates systemic inequality. These barriers occur in the same America where billionaires live lavish lifestyles, gain more wealth, and increase their power.

In order to confront these inequalities, Bower proposes a guaranteed minimum income. An idea deeply rooted in our nation's history, a guaranteed minimum income is a bipartisan idea of providing a cash payment to all Americans, no questions asked. A simple check for $1000 dollars a month can help Americans take more risks, pay off debt burdens, and build a freer, happier society.

Americans have suffered for far too long in a system that does not help the American people. Bower is laser focused on building an economy that works for everyone.
