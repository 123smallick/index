Issues

The United States of America is composed of unique, vibrant communities, but it is also one of the most unequal nations in the World– Across the country, the rich continue to get richer and the poorest americans continue stagnate. Our nations infrastructure and public transport are slowly wearing away. Tech billionaires mine are information and extort us from our own privacy

These disparities didn’t just appear. They are the result of countless years of overlooked, discriminatory policies that increased income inequalities and advantaged the most powerful people in our country. Bower's life work has been to reverse the impacts of these destructive policies by ensuring that the next generation of business people and politicians are brought up with a mindset of community, driving childrens' decisions to not benefit oneself but the whole community.

As president, Bower will fight the cruel, draconian policies put forward by previous adminstrations, which threaten to take us backwards, and is working everyday - in close partnership with the community - to advance bold, empathetic policy on issues of consequence to the people of America, from housing and transportation to healthcare, education, justice, and the rights of working families.

  • Confronting the climate crisis
  • Education by the People for the People
  • An economy that works for everyone
  • Transforming our criminal (in)justice system
